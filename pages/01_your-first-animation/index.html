Your First Animation

This is your first real GSAP animation, and it already shows what’s possible with just a few lines of code.

GSAP helps you create smooth, powerful animations that go beyond what regular CSS offers. You can control timing, easing, and even add logic for when and how things animate.

It’s fast, flexible, and trusted by companies like Apple, Google, and Nike to build beautiful interactions. Read more on the Official GSAP docs.
